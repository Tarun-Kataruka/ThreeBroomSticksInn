About Us

Welcome to The Three Broomsticks Inn, where culinary artistry meets a cozy ambiance and a celebration of cultural diversity. Our restaurant is a celebration of flavours, dedicated to bringing you an exceptional dining experience. With a passion for perfection, we craft each dish to delight your senses and leaving a lasting memory. From our expert chefs to our warm and attentive staffs, every aspect of our restaurant is designed to make your visit truly special.

Five-time winner of Best Upscale Restaurant in Karnataka Magazine's Restaurant Awards, The Three Broomsticks Inn is glamorous and elegant, yet relaxed and welcoming with warm and attentive service located in the heart of Karnataka "namma Bengaluru".
